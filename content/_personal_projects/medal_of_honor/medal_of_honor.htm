medal of honor: reimagined (2022)

Unreal Engine 5.0

"Medal of Honor: Reimagined" is my homage to one of my all-time favorite video games: Medal of Honor: Allied Assault (2002).

With the power and fidelity of Unreal Engine 5, I was able to recreate and remaster the Algerian town of Arzew, featured in the opening mission. I've taken creative and artistic liberties while still staying as close to the original as possible.

My work on "Medal of Honor" included:

  • Rebuilding the base geometry, using Unreal Engine 5's Modeling Tools
  • Asset integration and material creation
  • Full prop- and set dressing
  • Full lighting pass using Lumen and Ultra Dynamic Sky
  • Character integration, rigging and animating it using Control Rig
  • Implementing truck functionalities (lights and wheels), using Blueprint
  • Cinematic implementation using Unreal Engine's Level Sequencer
    • Camera flythrough
    • Truck movement

arzew

Medal of Honor: Allied Assault (2002) is a videogame with a special place my heart. Spending most of my teenage years with this game, I loved every minute of it.

I was curious if I would be able to make levels for it, so when the MoHTools arrived, I took my first shot at environment- and level design. And it got me hooked.

 

Fast forward 20 years or so later, I wanted to use my experience and knowledge to relive the memory, by creating a remastered / reimagined version of "Arzew", the opening mission. This mission depicted the chaos of World War II well, with your squad being ambushed around every turn.
