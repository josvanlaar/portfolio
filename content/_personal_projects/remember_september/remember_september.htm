remember september (2008)

call of duty world at war

 

Remember September started in 2006 as a personal project and grew to one of the biggest modifications for Call of Duty 2. Remember September was meant to be a single player experience, portraying the events of Operation Market Garden.
Due to other commitments, the project was sadly cancelled in June 2008.

My work on "Remember September" included:

  • Leading a team of ~15 developers
  • Initial research into the area, story, events, missions etc.
  • Developed four singleplayer levels "Nuenen", "Nijmegen Bridge" and "Grave"
  • Full prop- and set dressing + Lighting pass
  • Texture creation using Adobe Photoshop and textures.com
  • Collision pass
  • Sound and FX implementation
  • Dialogue implementation
  • Scripting using CoDScript (C based)
    • Mission & Objectives
    • AI Bot navigation implementation
